WHO IS AKSHAJ?

Akshaj Hegde is a Grade X 'B' student with a reputation that precedes him in every corridor of the school. Whether it's the classroom, the sports field, or a school event, Akshaj is the person others naturally look to — not because he demands it, but because he earns it.

Over his years at school, Akshaj has built a quiet, unshakable credibility. He combines academic diligence with genuine athletic ability, and layers both with a sense of responsibility toward his peers that makes him an instinctive leader.

As a candidate for Raven House Captain, Akshaj doesn't just represent the house — he embodies its values: collaborate, uplift, lead by example, and make an impact.
